The song **"Mannil Indha Kaadhal"** from the movie *Keladi Kanmani* (1990) is one of S.P. Balasubrahmanyam's (SPB) most iconic "breathless" performances. Composed by Maestro **Ilaiyaraaja**, the song is celebrated not just for SPB's technical feat but for its beautiful melodic structure.

### **1. Raga (Melodic Scale)**
The song is set in the **Keeravani** raga ([21st Melakarta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keeravani)). In Western music, this is equivalent to the **Harmonic Minor Scale**.

* **Mood:** Keeravani typically evokes feelings of longing, romance, and a touch of melancholy, which perfectly fits the theme of a mature love story in the film.
* **Scale Structure:**
    * **Arohanam (Ascending):** $S \ R_2 \ G_2 \ M_1 \ P \ D_1 \ N_3 \ \dot{S}$
    * **Avarohanam (Descending):** $\dot{S} \ N_3 \ D_1 \ P \ M_1 \ G_2 \ R_2 \ S$

### **2. Swaras (Musical Notes)**
If you are looking to play or understand the notes, the song's primary key is often identified as **B Minor** (though some tutorials use **C Minor** or **D Minor** for ease).

* **Pallavi Swaras (Sample):**
    * *Mannil Indha:* `P . . M G . . .`
    * *Kaadhal Andri:* `R S N S R`
    * The song utilizes the **Chathusruthi Rishabha ($R_2$)**, **Sadharana Gandhara ($G_2$)**, **Suddha Madhyama ($M_1$)**, **Suddha Dhaivatha ($D_1$)**, and **Kakali Nishadha ($N_3$)**.

### **3. The "Breathless" Charanam**
While it is famously known as a [breathless song](https://www.thenewsminute.com/tamil-nadu/singing-single-breath-radikaa-vasanth-recall-spbs-iconic-keladi-kanmani-song-133915), the "breathless" portion actually occurs during the **Charanams** (stanzas). 

* **Technique:** SPB sings the entire stanza, starting from *"Vennilavum ponni nadhiyum..."*, without an audible intake of breath. 
* **Fact Check:** While it sounds like one continuous breath, it is a masterclass in **air management** and **legato** singing. In the studio recording, SPB performed it in long takes to achieve that seamless effect, which he often replicated live to stun audiences.

### **4. Composition Details**
* **Composer:** [Ilaiyaraaja](https://www.raaga.com/tamil/songs/mannil-indha-kadhal-193)
* **Lyricist:** Gangai Amaran
* **Film:** *Keladi Kanmani* (Directed by Vasanth)
